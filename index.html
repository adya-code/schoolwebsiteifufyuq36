Welcome To Convent Of Jesus & Mary

The Convent of Jesus and Mary School, Ambala Cantt., an English Medium Girls Senior Secondary School established in 1909 under the Catholic Diocese of Agra is presently affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. The school has provision for the 10+2 scheme of education with approved facilities for the academic stream from LKG to XII. The academic year extends from April to March of the following year and is divided into two terms. Value based Education backed by scientific temperament and Catechetic for Christian students forms an integral part of the School Curriculum. The primary aim of the teaching faculty of the Convent of Jesus and Mary is to make God kn5every student of the School. Therefore, along with the prime concern of academic development of the pupils, the School lays emphasis for their spiritual awakening as well. Physical training, games and sports are compulsory activities. Students are trained in Table Tennis, Basketball, Throw-ball, Lawn Tennis, Volley ball, Badminton and skating for in-house and and Inter-School Competitions. Other fields in which girls can hone their skills are Social Service, Creative pursuits like Journalism, Computers, Environmental Preservation, House Keeping, Hindustani and Western Music etc. Students are also encouraged to actively participate in activities organized by the English Literary Club, Heritage Club, Dance club, Skating club and Choir club
